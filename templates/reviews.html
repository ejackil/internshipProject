Waste of Time and Money

☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆

October 5, 2023 - Luke Quilty

My experience at Ferret and Goose was a complete disaster from start to finish. We arrived for our reservation only to be told they had no record of it, despite my confirmation email. After waiting for 45 minutes, we were finally seated at a cramped table near the noisy kitchen. Our waiter was rude and inattentive, taking another 30 minutes to take our order. When the food eventually arrived, it was an utter mess. The Stuffed portobello mushrooms was burnt and inedible, and the Ferret Mushroom Risotto was cold and flavorless. To top it off, we found a hair in one of the dishes. We asked to speak with the manager, who was dismissive and unapologetic. The final insult was the exorbitant bill, which they refused to adjust despite the horrible experience. We left feeling utterly disappointed and ripped off. Avoid this place at all costs!
